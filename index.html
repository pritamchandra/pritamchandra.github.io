Hi, Pritam here. This space here is to host a work portfolio and a blog. Please get in touch if either of these are of interest to you. My social-links are available at the footer of this page.

  • Professional

On the vocation front, the area of my primary interest is the theory of matrices and its plentiful connection to several corners of mathematics and computer science. I have been fortunate to be trained by Prof. Rajendra Bhatia during my undergrad and PG Diploma at Ashoka University. In my final year I wrote a thesis on inequalities related to the Cartesian decomposition of matrices.

More recently, I have worked at Microsoft Research and Mphasis. At Microsoft I’ve worked on smoothed analysis of algorithms appearing in Unsupervised Learning, and on the gradient flow of Machine Learning architectures. At Mphasis, I have studied and reviewed the state-of-the-art lattice based signature schemes with a focus on Falcon. Underneath these projects there has often been a curious problem of a matrix flavor. Please refer to my resume for further information.

I consider myself a perpetual student of Mathematics and its neighbors. I look forward to relishing newer and deeper structures and make meaningful contribution in theory and practice. I will document portions of my work and my study in this blog.
